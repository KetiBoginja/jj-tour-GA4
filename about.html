About me

Who am I?

I am not an ordinary Tour Guide.

This does mean that I am a Ministry of Tourism approved licensed Tour Guide.

This also means that I am not a walking Wikipedia or a human version of Tripadvisor. I am a Tour Educator. I am focused on your growth as a person, I wish to grow with you. I am entertaining, interactive and fun; I don’t just stand and speak at a group, I talk to my fellow travellers. However this is not just ‘Info-tainment’. My aim is that by introducing you to my home, Seychelles, that you will have an encounter with the human and natural history of this region that will help you grow as a person. What form this growth takes, well, that you have control over. Please remember being involved in Education is not limited to any age or religion, family situation or ethnicity, it is open to all. Above all else Education should be fun!

As well as being a tour educator I am a husband and father, I live in Victoria in a neighbourhood called Beau vallon. I love gardening, cooking and entertaining friends.

What can do I do for you?

As a professionally licensed Seychelles Tour Guide and I can guide any part of Seycheles for any group. Let me take you on tours of the of Mahe, La Gigue and Pralin. We can hike in National Parks, scuba diving and kayaking. I can arrange birding tours, culinary tours, political tours and much more. I will arrange accommodation, transport and site entrance to all parts of this wonderful land for you so that you can have a trouble free holiday. Let me make your tour of Seychelles unique!

If you have a group with an educational focus I can facilitate learning on a broad variety of subjects including; ‘Knowledge of the Land of Seychelles’, our philosophy and history.

Are you looking for a family tour guide? I am a educator at the American International School and have experience creating fun activities for kids of all ages.

I do not guide ordinary tours, I guide extraordinary tours, I wish to give you a unique experience, an alternative tour of Seychelles.
