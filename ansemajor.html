Surf & Turf - Anse Major + Marine Park

Journey to breathtaking Anse Major, by boat or by foot, and then set sail to Baie Ternay Marine Park!

This tour departs on Wednesdays and Saturdays from Mahe only.

What is included:

  • BBQ lunch on-board
  • Entrance fees to Baie Ternay Marine Park
  • Transfers to and from your hotel

What to bring:

  • Light, comfortable clothes
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimwear / Hat
  • Sunscreen / Beach Towel
  • Mosquito repellent
  • Sunglasses
  • Camera
  • Cash or credit card for beverages
  • Snorkelling equipment
  • Bottle of water

NOTE:This tour is not recommended for pregnant women and the physically challenged. Infants under 2 years of age are not permitted.

Details

This tour will take you to the beautiful Anse Major, a majestic, breathtaking and untouched paradise. And the best is, both land and sea will take you to this natural revelation! You can choose between an adventurous hike or a cruise along the coast!

For the adventurous, the trek offers a 360-degree panoramic view of Seychelles' most breathtaking scenery. The granite boulders, white sand and thick tree canopies depict a picturesque landscape.

The cruise is a truly relaxing way to get to Anse Major. Providing the opportunity to take in the fresh sea air and stunning coastal views.

Once in Anse Major, you will enjoy the symbiosis of all its natural assets. You will be able to go for a swim before boarding the Odezir catamaran and set sail to the famous Baie Ternay Marine Park, where you will enjoy a mouthwatering BBQ lunch on board. You will then have the whole afternoon to relax, swim and even snorkel. This is your time to discover Seychelles' stunning underwater world.

The tour starts from Danzilles, Bel Ombre and ends at Creole Marina on Mahe. The tour operator offers free hotel pick-up and drop-off.
