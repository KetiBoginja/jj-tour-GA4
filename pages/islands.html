Two Island Melody Tour - from Mahe

Take a guided tour through the gorgeous islands La Digue and Praslin.

Visit Vallee de Mai and take a picture with the famous Coco de Mer!

This tour currently operates twice a week, on Mondays and Thursdays, and can be booked from Praslin. Boat transfers from Mahe, or La Digue, to Praslin, can be booked through our website separately.

What is included:
  • Hotel pick-up/drop-off
  • Guided tour
  • Transfers to and from your hotel
  • Ferry crossing to Praslin and La Digue
  • Lunch and bottled water
  • Visit to UNESCO Nature reserve Vallee de Mai (Praslin)
  • Visit legendary Anse Lazio Beach (Praslin)
  • Visit to L'Union Estate (La Digue)
  • Visit to fabulous Anse Source D'Argent Beach (La Digue)

What to bring:Light casual clothing, swimwear, comfortable walking shoes, sun cream, beach towel, and a camera.

Details

Explore the most iconic landmarks of Seychelles by booking a guided tour from Mahe to visit Praslin and La Digue. Your guide will show you the most intriguing places on the islands, including the mysterious Vallee de Mai - home to the legendary Coco de Mer. This UNESCO Nature Reserve - often compared to the Garden of the Eden - is a must-see, and this tour will take you through the mystical jungle and pathways filled with history and myths. You’ll be able to admire the endemic bird species and get a real feel of the local culture when you visit a national museum of Praslin.

You will also visit L’Union Estate (La Digue), a national heritage site, where you can expect to see a traditional copra mill, meet the giant tortoises and visit the old plantation house. Moreover, you will visit the most popular beach in Seychelles - Anse Source D’Argent, one of the most photographed beaches in the world! Here you will have time to relax at the beach and have a refreshing dip in the turquoise sea.

Feel free to contact us if anything remains unclear, and we'd be more than happy to help you out.
