Scuba Diving for Beginners

Take your first breaths underwater

Discover Mahe's pristine waters and experience the island like never before!

Blue Sea Divers is considered one of Mahe's premier diving centres and is ideally located in the heart of Beau Vallon on Mahe Island. The dive centre, offering SSI, PADI and CMAS certifications, was born through the passion and hard work of its owner - Sylvain (Master instructor 3 Stars PADI OWDI) - whose wish was to offer diving in a professional, safe and enjoyable manner to those visiting Seychelles. Together with his qualified, multi-lingual team, they will help you have the experience of a lifetime while always making sure you safely enjoy your time on and under the water.

Intro Dive - No prior experience with scuba diving is required!

For the complete beginner, Blue Sea Divers offer an Introduction to Scuba Diving, allowing you to experience the incredible underwater world first-hand. You'll start with a brief introduction given by a qualified diving instructor. Then, equipped with the necessary knowledge, you'll be taken on a dive to a maximum depth of 7 meters, where a great variety of marine life awaits you. You might come across mantas, rays, turtles, octopus, sharks, whale sharks, dolphins, butterflyfish and many other fish species!

And, if one dive isn't enough, it's possible to book a Twin Tank (2 dives) introduction!

IMPORTANT: All bookings are 'On Request' and subject to availability. Blue Sea Divers will contact you with the available times for your chosen date.

Included in this adventure:
  • Qualified dive instructors
  • Diving equipment
  • Certificate of Attendance (this is not an official document or scuba license)
  • Transport from dive centre to dive site if required
  • 1 dive or 2 dives (in one day)
  • Free pick up from Beau Vallon area hotels

NOTE: When applicable, the Marine Park entrance fee (€17) is not included in the price

What to bring: Towel, swimwear, and sunscreen

Minimum age: 10 years old
