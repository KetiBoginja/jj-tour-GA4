Vallee de Mai

Take a guided tour through the gorgeous Vallée de Mai, Nature Reserve on Praslin.

Visit Vallee de Mai and take a picture with the famous Coco de Mer!

This tour currently operates twice a week, on Mondays and Thursdays, and can be booked from Praslin. Boat transfers from Mahe, or La Digue, to Praslin, can be booked through our website separately.

What is included:
  • Hotel pick-up/drop-off
  • Guided tour
  • Transfers to and from your hotel
  • Park Entry Fees

What to bring:Light casual clothing, comfortable walking shoes, sun cream, cash and a camera.

Details

Enter the mysterious UNESCO World Heritage Site of Vallée de Mai, located in the centre of Praslin Island. This nature reserve boasts an endemic palm forest, bearing the famous “Coco de Mer”, double-lobed Coconut, shaped like a woman’s pelvis. The valley is home to an array of exotic wildlife such as black parrot, frogs, chameleons, small mammals, giant snails, and crustaceans. Your guide will show you first-hand the different species and will explain how the forest came to be. Legend has it that this was once the biblical Garden of Eden. This forest, with its peculiar fauna and flora, is definitely worth a visit!

Feel free to contact us if anything remains unclear, and we'd be more than happy to help you out.
